About me

Hello, I'm Stephanie, Front-End Web Developer and Photographer based in Toronto, Canada. I focus on building beautiful, clean, responsive web pages.

Prior to completing the accelerated Web Development program and Web Development Immersive (Bootcamp) at HackerYou, I worked as a Dental Assistant for 10 years, where I was able to work closely on a team to deliver exceptional patient care. I love problem solving and keeping up to date with the latest design trends and am always looking for exciting new opportunities to learn and grow my skill set. My attention to detail and eye for design make a great combination in what I do.

Dabbling in photography, I specialize in animal and street photography. My two main subjects of attention are Olive and Tofu, my 7 and 3 year old French Bulldogs! We are all happiest in the sunshine at one of the off-leash dog parks or beach here in Toronto. When not behind the computer screen or lens, you can find me honing my skills on one of the many outdoor rinks playing hockey or in one of my favorite ping pong social clubs, SPiN, destroying the competition.
